Welcome to Kwix

Kwix is a simple employee + payroll management system designed for small business that wish to go paperless at a low cost.

Kwix is a pay once, user forever App. No Subscriptions! No Fees!

This is a demo version available to the public and is still a work in progress.

This is a public ALPHA build and is not representative of the final product.

DEMO Features:

  • Employee Management:
      • - Create/Update/Delete Employees
      - Set Employee Pay
  • Payroll Management:
      • - Create/Update/Delete Paystubs For existing Employees
      - Manage hours worked for each employee
      - Calculate total pay (hours worked x employee hourly pay)
      - View gross earnings for Employee

    Interested In Full Access?

      - Paid users have access to save their data.
      - Save employee paystub history
      - Download paystubs records for employees that work with any spreadsheet software (microsoft excel included)
      - And more
